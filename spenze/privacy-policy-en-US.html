Privacy Policy

App: Spenze  |  Company: Mehnic  |  Last updated: April 25, 2026

Summary: Spenze collects only the data necessary to provide our personal finance management service with AI features. We do not sell your data. You can request deletion at any time.

1. Who We Are

The Spenze app is developed and operated by Mehnic, a company that owns a portfolio of mobile applications. For the purposes of applicable privacy laws (including Brazil's LGPD, the EU GDPR, and California's CCPA), Mehnic is the data controller for your personal information.

Data Protection Officer (DPO) / Privacy Contact:
Email: fabio.almcosta@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account and Profile Data

  • Full name — provided during registration
  • Email address — for authentication and communication
  • Profile photo — optional, uploaded by you
  • Language and currency preferences — for interface personalization
  • Financial goal — e.g., save money, pay off debt (set during onboarding)

2.2 Financial Data

  • Transactions — amount, date, description, category (income or expense)
  • Budgets — spending limits per category and period
  • Custom categories — created by you

This data is stored on our secure servers (Azure PostgreSQL) and is visible only to you.

2.3 Voice Data

When you use the voice transaction feature, your audio is transmitted to Microsoft Azure Cognitive Services Speech-to-Text for transcription. Audio is not permanently stored on our systems after processing. The transcribed text is then used only to extract transaction details.

2.4 Social Authentication

If you sign in with Google, Facebook, or Apple:

  • We receive your name and email as provided by the social provider
  • We do not store your access tokens or passwords from those providers
  • Each provider has its own Privacy Policy: Google, Facebook/Meta, Apple

2.5 Subscription Data

  • Plan status (free or premium)
  • Subscription expiry date and type
  • Processed via RevenueCat — actual payment is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play and never passes through our servers

2.6 Technical and Usage Data

  • Device language and OS (iOS or Android)
  • Messages sent to the AI financial assistant (chat feature)
  • App crash and error logs (no personal financial data included)

Some of our providers are located outside Brazil and the European Union (primarily in the United States). We ensure such transfers are made with adequate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and compliance programs (e.g., EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework), as required by LGPD Art. 33 and GDPR Chapter V.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data and transactions: retained while your account is active
  • After account deletion: all personal data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days
  • Voice audio: not stored — processed in real-time and immediately discarded
  • Technical logs: up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes
  • Legal obligations: some data may be retained longer if required by applicable law

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

For all users (LGPD — Brazil):

  • Access: know what data we hold about you
  • Correction: update inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: request deletion of data processed with your consent, or unnecessary data — available directly in the app at Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Data portability: receive your data in structured format (CSV) — available at Settings → Export Data
  • Revocation of consent: withdraw consent at any time for consent-based processing
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • ANPD complaint: you may file a complaint with the Brazilian National Data Protection Authority (gov.br/anpd)

For EU/EEA users (GDPR):

  • All rights above plus: restriction of processing and right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority

For California users (CCPA):

  • Right to know, delete, and opt out of sale (we do not sell data). You may also request non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at fabio.almcosta@gmail.com. We respond within 15 business days.

8. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All communication encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords stored using secure hashing (bcrypt)
  • Database hosted in a restricted-access environment (Azure PostgreSQL)
  • Authentication via JWT tokens with automatic expiration
  • Automatic database backups with encryption at rest

No system is completely immune to security threats. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable law.

9. Children's Privacy

Spenze is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). If we become aware that a minor's data has been collected, we will take immediate steps to delete it. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us at fabio.almcosta@gmail.com.

10. Cookies and Tracking

Spenze is a mobile application and does not use cookies. We do not use advertising trackers, cross-app tracking, or behavioral profiling for third-party purposes.

11. Push Notifications

With your permission, we may send push notifications for financial alerts and reminders. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings or in the app under Settings → Notifications.

12. App Store Data Practices (Apple)

In compliance with Apple App Store requirements, here is a summary of our data practices:

  • Data used to track you: None
  • Data linked to you: Contact info (email, name), Financial info (transactions, budgets), User content (profile photo), Identifiers (user ID)
  • Data not linked to you: Usage data for app functionality diagnostics
