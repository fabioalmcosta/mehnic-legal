When you log in to Spenze using Facebook, we store only your name and email address. No other Facebook data is collected or stored.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

  1. 1Open the Spenze app
  2. 2Go to Settings (bottom navigation)
  3. 3Tap Account
  4. 4Tap Delete Account and confirm
All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Option 2 — Request by email

No longer have access to the app? Send a deletion request including the email associated with your account.

Request deletion by email

Ao fazer login no Spenze com o Facebook, armazenamos apenas seu nome e endereco de e-mail. Nenhum outro dado do Facebook e coletado ou armazenado.

Todos os dados pessoais serao excluidos permanentemente em ate 30 dias.
