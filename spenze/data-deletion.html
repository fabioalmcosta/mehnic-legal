Data Deletion — Spenze

How to delete your account and personal data from Spenze

When you log in to Spenze using Facebook, we store only your name and email address to create your account. No other Facebook data is collected or stored.

Option 1 — Delete directly in the app

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the Spenze app:

  1. 1 Open the Spenze app on your device
  2. 2 Go to Settings (bottom navigation)
  3. 3 Tap Account
  4. 4 Tap Delete Account and confirm
Your account and all personal data (transactions, budgets, preferences) will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you no longer have access to the app, send us a deletion request by email. Include the email address associated with your Spenze account.

✉️ Request deletion by email

We will process your deletion request within 15 business days and send a confirmation email. For questions, contact fabio.almcosta@gmail.com.
